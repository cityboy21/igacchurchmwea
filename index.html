About Us

Our History

International Gospel Acts Church (IGAC) Mwea began as a small fellowship in 2025 with a vision to “raise a generation that worships relentlessly”. Over the period, we have grown to a family of over 500 members, impacting communities across Kenya.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To make disciples who love God, love people, and love the world.

Vision: A generation empowered by the Holy Spirit to transform nations.
